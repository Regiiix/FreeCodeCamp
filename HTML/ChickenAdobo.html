Chicken Adobo

Chicken Adobo is a popular Filipino dish made with chicken cooked in a savory and tangy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Brown sugar
  • Bay leaves
  • Black peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chicken pieces and cook until browned.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, brown sugar, bay leaves, and black peppercorns.
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens and the chicken is cooked through.
